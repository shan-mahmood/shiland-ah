Meet your veterinarian

Dr. Sabah Kadhim

Owner & Veterinarian · Serving Rock Hill since 2024

Dr. Kadhim opened Shiland Animal Hospital in October 2024 with one goal: give the pets of Rock Hill a place where experienced, personal care is available when families actually need it — not weeks out, and not only 9 to 5.

"Your community is our community. We want to be here for you and your pets when you need us the most."

His background spans small-animal medicine and emergency care, with a hands-on interest in soft-tissue surgery and urgent treatment — which is why Shiland handles cases in-house that many clinics refer out.

When every minute matters

An injured or very sick pet? Call us first.

With an in-house lab and advanced X-ray, we diagnose fast and act faster. Our facility is equipped for emergency surgery for cats and dogs — so in many cases, your pet can be treated right here.

Emergency surgeries we perform on-site

  • Exploratory surgery
  • Foreign body removal
  • Splenectomy
  • C-sections
  • Laceration repair
  • Pyometra surgery

Call ahead if you can — even a two-minute heads-up lets our team prepare before you arrive.
