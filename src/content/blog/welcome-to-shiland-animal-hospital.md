Shiland Animal Hospital opened its doors in October 2024 with a simple goal: give the pets of Rock Hill a place where experienced, personal care is available when families actually need it.

## Care that fits real life

Pets don't get sick on a schedule. That's why we're open six days a week — including evenings and weekends — with walk-ins welcome and same-day appointments available. Whether it's a routine wellness exam or a sudden emergency, you don't have to wait for Monday or drive to Charlotte.

## Everything under one roof

With an in-house lab, digital X-ray, and a surgical suite, we can diagnose quickly and treat many conditions right here in Rock Hill — from vaccines and dentistry to spay/neuter and emergency surgery.

## Come say hello

New to Shiland? Your pet's first wellness exam is on us. Walk in or call **(803) 752-4950** to claim it — we'd love to meet you and your pet.
